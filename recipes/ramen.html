SIMPLE RAMEN

Details

Simple Egg Ramen with Vegetables: This recipe creates a quick and flavorful homemade ramen with a soft-boiled egg and your choice of vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1 package instant ramen noodles (any flavor)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic (optional)
  • choice of vegetables:
  • Chopped green onions
  • Shredded carrots
  • Sliced baby spinach
  • Bean sprouts
  • Chopped mushrooms
  • Frozen peas
  • Other vegetables of your preference

After this, lets start the Instructions

  1. Boil the water: In a pot, bring the water to a boil.
  2. Cook the noodles: Add the ramen noodles to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions (usually 3-5 minutes).
  3. Prepare the egg: While the noodles cook, prepare your desired soft-boiled egg. For a slightly runny yolk, cook for 6-7 minutes. For a firmer yolk, cook for 8-9 minutes. Immediately transfer the egg to an ice bath to stop the cooking process.
  4. Make the broth: While the egg cools, create your broth. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, miso paste (if using), sesame oil, ginger (if using), and garlic (if using). Mix well.
  5. Assemble the ramen: Once the noodles are cooked, drain them and add them back to the pot. Pour in the prepared broth and stir to combine.
  6. Peel and add the egg: Peel the cooled egg and gently add it to the ramen.
  7. Add vegetables: Add your chosen vegetables to the ramen. If using fresh vegetables, cook them in the broth for a few minutes until tender. If using frozen vegetables, add them just before serving and allow them to heat through.
  8. Serve and enjoy!: Divide the ramen into bowls and garnish with additional toppings like chopped green onions, sesame seeds, or chili flakes.
